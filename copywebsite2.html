we are

Kalimat Group International FZ LLC has placed itself firmly on the map of Middle- Eastern media since 2002. Along with its professional communication experience, KGI developed into a strategic content marketing company, a one-stop shop for all your digital, social content, marketing and experiential needs, all produced and delivered in-house.

We provide integrated services based on a multistructural approach that includes the relevant content, platforms and technology. We provide transformational value and measurable business impact.

We offer a model in which we act as an extension to our partners, learning everything about their product, industry and customers in order to contribute to their growth. We tailor bespoke integrated digital, creative, experiential and content marketing strategies for each and every client with a unique approach, giving a particular voice to the brand across multiple social channels and related technologies.

KGI has 17 years record of success and steady growth, empowered by an impressive portfolio of large and diverse client base. Ultimately, we commit to delivering the promise.

LEADERSHIP



Hekmat el Zein

CEO, HEAD OF STRATEGY

Hekmat oversees all group strategic and operational activities.
He is graduate of Political Science from Concordia University, Montreal, Canada & the Lebanese American University, Beirut. In 1996, he became the Editor-in-Chief of Hurriyat monthly in the Rene Moawad Foundation. With a group of professional content creators and marketers, Hekmat launched KGI in 2002.
With his long practical international and regional experience, Hekmat is now one of the very few leading consultants on content marketing in the MENA region.

Shatha Sharaffdeen

GM, HEAD OF CONTENT

Shatha assumes the overall responsibility in supervising the content production and social media strategy implementation in KGI since 2012.
A graduate from the faculty of Arts & Sciences in the American University of Beirut in 1998, Shatha was the Editor-in-Chief of the Arabic family magazine “AHALI”. She worked with sevral international and regional brands and she is continuously attending international training courses on social content production and content marketing.
Shatha joined KGI in 2002.

Adam Scott

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT DIRECTOR

Adam supervises the regional business development operation with a focus on UAE & KSA. He studied in Wymondham, England and started his career in the online publishing in 2010, as an online business development manager at Schofield Publishing, Norwich.
In 2018, he moved in from Startle International in London to Dubai to join Eat App as a Business Development Expert.
He left an impressive record in his career path and he is presently investing his skills in KGI. Adam Joined KGI in 2019.

Casilda Cordeiro

DIGITAL ACCOUNT MANAGER

Casilda looks after client needs and digital strategy execution. her expertise has evolved over the past 12 years in the UAE from ground-level consumer service to strategically managing & executing high-impact marketing campaigns.
She graduated with Advance Honors in International Marketing Management with a Degree in Marketing & Advertising from Madonna University, UK. Her career includes working with Zee TV Middle East, Fatafeat TV – Discovery Networks.
